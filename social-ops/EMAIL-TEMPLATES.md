# Prelaunch email templates

These templates stay inside the same double-opt-in list. Replace braces only with sourced daily facts or
verified product-manifest fields. Every send still requires the exact-copy approval batch.

## 1. Welcome after confirmation

**Subject:** Welcome to the TraderCockpit market map

You’re confirmed.

TraderCockpit separates three things that market coverage often blurs: the confirmed fact, the mechanism
that can transmit it into portfolios, and the scenario that would need a trigger. Expect concise market
maps built from primary releases, with source links, as-of times, and visible corrections.

Cadence: daily event coverage when the evidence is strong, plus a weekly “what actually changed” recap.
The product is still a waitlist concept; we will not announce capabilities, availability, or pricing until
they are verified by the consumer product.

This is research and education, not individualized financial advice. Reply with the market question you
want sourced next. You can unsubscribe at any time.
