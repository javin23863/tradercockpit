## 2 Why did it move?

Two mechanisms braided. (a) Policy deadline: after 12:01am EDT today no Iranian oil or petrochemical transaction is authorized — the compliance floor under the oil market's war premium hardens on a date certain, while the physical route is already carrying a blockade and near-zero transits. (b) Leadership divergence: the market sold record AI-chip earnings — when the best possible print cannot lift its own stock, the marginal buyer of expensive growth is exhausted and the money looks for a floor elsewhere. Energy closing at highs on the same day the Nasdaq broke support is that rotation showing up on completed bars, not in commentary. The map is an inference, not a claim that either event caused every tick.
