why a machine that kills strategies

No lying allowed

Nothing on the deck is ever synthesized to look good. If Monte-Carlo paths weren't recorded, the panel says "MC PATHS NOT RECORDED" — it doesn't draw pretty fakes. Absence is stated, never decorated.

Evidence or it didn't happen

"SURVIVOR" status requires a finished run with provenance-matched receipts. Fresh strategies read UNTESTED. The words "validated" and "robust" are locked behind cleared gates — they cannot appear otherwise.

Paper-only by design

TraderCockpit is a research instrument, not a bot. Order routing is hard-blocked in code. It exists to tell you whether an edge is real — not to promise you one.

The gurus' incentive is to convince you their strategy works. Our machine's only job is to prove yours doesn't — and fail. When it can't kill a strategy after 12 phases, that survival means something.

what actually runs inside

Genesis funnel

Thousands of strategy variants enter a gated pipeline. Each phase is a filter with teeth: data integrity, parameter plateaus, walk-forward, resampled and shuffled Monte-Carlo, cost stress, regime splits, alpha decay.

MC theater

The same deterministic simulations the gates graded, drawn as spaghetti clouds — your equity curve against a hundred alternate universes. If your curve only wins in the universe you backtested, you'll see it.

Honest ledger

Every candidate ever bred is counted — including the ones culled before admission. Odometers run off the ledger census, not marketing math.
