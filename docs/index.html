Write the actual rules

A video title is not a strategy specification. Entry, exit, risk, market, timeframe, and unavailable details must become explicit before a result can mean anything.

Preview is not a verdict

A signal sketch can expose broken or silent logic. Only the full battery may produce a robustness verdict, and Apollo must ask before launching that run.
