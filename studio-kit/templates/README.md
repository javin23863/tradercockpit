# 🎨 Motion-graphics templates

These are the AI-themed animation styles the app renders for your intros. Each is a
glowing, depth-rich 3D scene designed to *read as AI* — not a flat stock preset.

| Template | Look |
|---|---|
| **Neural Net** | A clean feed-forward network: layers of nodes, an activation wave sweeping bottom→top, signal pulses traveling along the edges. Legible "this is AI." |
| **Neural Cortex** | A dense volumetric brain-like cloud: hundreds of nodes, ~1,500 connections, traveling pulses, drifting dust, fog depth. Cinematic and complex. |

**How they render:** the app drives the motion-graphics engine to render each scene
frame-by-frame into video, with depth-of-field, controlled bloom (glow), and film grain —
the details that separate real motion design from a screensaver.

> This folder documents the AI motion-graphics scene styles in the kit. The render pipeline
> and how to drive it are covered in `STUDIO-PROCESS.md`.
