model released by Meta under **Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial 4.0 (CC BY-NC 4.0)**.

What that license means:
- ✅ Free to use for **personal evaluation, learning, and research**.
- ❌ **Not** for paid/commercial services, and the model weights may not be resold.

So the scorer is **installed from its official source on first run** (one click) — you
always get the genuine, latest model, and you accept Meta's free non-commercial license
directly as the end user. The integration code in this kit is ours and included.

**The honest line:** make and monetize videos freely with the *creation* tools. Use the
*scorer* for evaluating and improving your own content, not as a paid commercial service.
